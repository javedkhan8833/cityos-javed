## 4) Ownership / Overlap Decision Matrix (No Confusion, No Duplication)

### 4.1 Who owns what (system of record)

| Domain Object                          | System of Record                     | Why                  | Other Systems Role                     |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Editorial POI content, pages, blocks   | **Payload CMS**                      | content lifecycle    | ERP references only                    |
| Orders, carts, checkout                | **Medusa**                           | transaction engine   | ERP receives postings                  |
| Payments execution (gateway)           | **Medusa/Wallet**                    | real-time payments   | ERP mirrors/accounting                 |
| Invoices, settlements, payouts batches | **ERPNext**                          | financial truth      | Medusa shows status                    |
| Procurement (PR/RFQ/PO)                | **ERPNext**                          | governance & audit   | CMS can display                        |
| Vendor commercial storefront profile   | **Payload/Commerce**                 | marketplace UX       | ERP is vendor compliance/finance truth |
| Inventory truth                        | **ERPNext** (or dedicated WMS later) | accounting & control | Commerce consumes availability         |
| HR, payroll, cost centers              | **ERPNext**                          | org truth            | other systems reference                |
| Approvals & long-running processes     | **Temporal**                         | execution truth      | ERP/CMS/Commerce are state stores      |
| Moderation escalation                  | **Temporal + Moderation system**     | HIL workflows        | CMS consumes decisions                 |

### 4.2 “Hard rule” to prevent future chaos

* **ERP records money, liabilities, settlements, payouts, and procurement approvals.**
* **Medusa executes customer commerce and real-time payment flow.**
* **Payload expresses content, catalog presentation, and editorial state.**
* **Temporal orchestrates all long-running business processes and approvals.**
